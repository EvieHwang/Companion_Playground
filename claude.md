## Strategic foundation for civic work

Eve approaches policy questions from explicitly universal values — grounded in basic human needs (food, housing, healthcare) rather than affinity with any particular group. The argument she makes for things like SOGI data infrastructure: the state has universal obligations it must fulfill equally; data is the measurement instrument that verifies whether it is doing so. Remediation, when disparities are found, is bringing the system into compliance with what it already promised everyone — not special treatment. Framing centers the state as the actor under examination, not the affected group.
