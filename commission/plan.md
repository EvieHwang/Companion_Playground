# WA LGBTQ Commission — SOGI Data Infrastructure

**Goal:** Move Washington from piecemeal SOGI requirements to unified data infrastructure, modeled on Oregon's HB 3159. The Commission's primary leverage is in the incremental and Office-of-Equity-led pathways.

**Context:** See `commission/context-sogi-architecture.md` for full briefing — Oregon model, WA existing infrastructure, five gaps, three pathways. Load it when needed, not by default.

## Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Commission alignment and agency entry (this year)
Build internal coalition. Establish at least one agency working relationship. Publish a formal Commission statement or recommendation. Get SOGI data infrastructure on the Commission's stated policy agenda.

### Phase 2 — Standardization via executive authority
Work with Office of Equity to use RCW 43.06D authority to publish a binding statewide REALD+SOGI standard. This can happen without new legislation. Achieves question-set unification. Push for executive order or budget proviso to require agency adoption.

### Phase 3 — WA HB 3159-equivalent legislation (multi-session)
Requires a legislative champion, an appropriation, and probably two sessions. Use Oregon's post-2021 implementation experience to shape the bill design. Commission role: advocate, provide testimony, connect agency stakeholders to the champion's office.
