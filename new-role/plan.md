# Job Search Plan — May 2026

## Where we are

Amazon contract wrapping in a couple of weeks. Runway is 4–6 months between $20K savings + unemployment + ShinShin's income + family backstop. Not urgent enough to panic, not loose enough to be casual. Active applying starts now.

Search is at zero. Resume and LinkedIn exist but haven't been audited for whether they tell the integrated story. No conversations started, no target list, no network activated.

That's fine. We start clean.

## Strategy

**Target.** Senior PM (L6) as the floor. Principal IC or Director/Head of Product at smaller mission-aligned orgs as the upside — worth holding open. Strategic problem-solving role with supply chain, analytics, data systems, demand forecasting, or food security as the substrate. AI as a tool, not the focus.

**The filter that screens for fit.** Real people, real problems, *and* leadership stability with direct sponsorship.

The pattern across Slalom, Nordstrom, and Amazon: you drive things to clean and finished, then get cut off by leadership change or political churn before the work fully lands. That's not a you problem — it's a fit problem. The fix is structural. Choose environments where the person backing you is the person setting the direction, not a layer between you and them that can change its mind. Smaller mission-aligned orgs solve for this by default. Bigger orgs solve for it occasionally, when the right manager is in the right seat — riskier bet, but possible.

**Posture.** Selective, not desperate. The runway buys you the right to hold the line on fit. Use it.

Looking for a problem worth getting your teeth into — complex, challenging, uses lots of different skills. A company with enough going on to be interesting. A manager who trusts you to take on something tricky.

This pitch needs another pass once we've talked about which threads to emphasize for which audience. The line "it disappears" is doing real work — keep it.

4. **Network seed list.** The first 3–5 people you'd ping if I told you to start activating tomorrow. Not asks for jobs — asks for conversations.

Bring all four to the next session.
